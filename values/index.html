WHAT
IS CALLED VALUE ?

In Islam values encompass a wide range of virtues and principles that guide the lives of Muslims. Key values include honesty, compassion, justice, patience, and trustworthiness. These values are rooted in the Quran and the teachings of the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH), and they serve as a moral compass, shaping individual behavior and fostering a just and harmonious society.

Here's a more detailed look at some of these core Islamic values:

  1. Honesty: Honesty is a fundamental principle in Islam, emphasizing truthfulness in speech and action. Islamic sources say it is crucial for building trust and maintaining integrity in all aspects of life.
  2. Compassion: Compassion and mercy are highly valued in Islam. Muslims are encouraged to show kindness and empathy towards all beings, including animals.
  3. Justice: Islam emphasizes the importance of fairness and impartiality in all dealings. Islamic teachings state that justice should be upheld, even when it is difficult or unpopular.
  4. Patience: Patience is considered a virtue in Islam, especially when facing adversity or difficulty. Muslims are encouraged to remain steadfast and persevere through trials with grace.
  5. Trustworthiness: Muslims are expected to be reliable and dependable, keeping their promises and fulfilling their obligations.
  6. Forgiveness: Islam teaches the importance of forgiveness, both towards others and oneself. According to Islamic teachings, forgiving others is a way to purify the heart and promote peace.
  7. Respect: Respect for elders, parents, and those in positions of authority is also a core Islamic value.
  8. Generosity: Islam encourages Muslims to be generous and charitable, giving to those in need and contributing to the well-being of society.
  9. 9. Humility: Humility is valued as a way to recognize the greatness of God and to avoid arrogance and pride.
These values are not just abstract concepts but are intended to be lived out in daily life, shaping the character of individuals and contributing to a more just and compassionate world.
